Diploma - Engineering Branches

Pursue a 3-year diploma after 10th SSLC across diverse engineering fields

Career Pathway

10th SSLC
→
Diploma (3 Years)
→
Job / Lateral Entry to B.E.
→
Career

Engineering Branches

  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Information Science
  • Automobile Engineering
  • Mechatronics Engineering
  • Robotics & Automation
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Tool & Die Engineering
  • Instrumentation Technology
  • Chemical Engineering

Admission Process

  • Eligibility: 10th SSLC Pass
  • DCET (Diploma Common Entrance Test)
  • Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) Counseling
  • Government & Private Polytechnic Colleges
  • Merit-Based Direct Admission
  • Lateral Entry to 2nd Year B.E./B.Tech

Career Outcomes

  • Core Engineering Jobs (Site Engineer, Production, Maintenance)
  • IT & Software Careers (Developer, Tester, Support Engineer)
  • Government Jobs (PWD, Railways, BESCOM, KPTCL)
  • Diploma to Engineering (Lateral Entry to B.E/B.Tech)
  • Industrial & Manufacturing Careers (Factories, Automation, CNC)
  • Entrepreneurship (Contractor, Workshop, Service Center)
  • Specialized Technical Roles (Automation, Robotics, Tool & Die)
  • Overseas & Skilled Technician Opportunities
